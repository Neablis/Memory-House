Memory House: a solo percussion performance by Peter Ferry

Memory House is a multimedia collaboration dealing with the distinctive feelings we give our own memories.

The project involves the Eastman School of Music, Rochester Institute of Technology, and you!

Visit http://memoryhouse.cias.rit.edu/ to upload a Polaroid image or photograph which evokes a memory, the feeling of nostalgia for you, or a response to the music. Both your image and your recorded spoken commentary will be included as source material for the performance. During the performance the audience members will be able to share their emotions via text messaging.
